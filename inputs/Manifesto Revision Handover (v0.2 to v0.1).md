## Context

We have an existing manifesto (v0.2) that blends:

* Philosophy
* Operating model
* Business model
* Service descriptions
* Internal notes

This creates **power + confusion at the same time**.

**Goal of this revision:**

> Transform the current document into a **true manifesto** — focused on worldview, identity, and non-negotiables — while extracting all business/operational elements into separate documents.

---

## 🎯 Objective for BMAD Agents

You are tasked to:

1. **Refactor the current manifesto into a pure manifesto (v1.0)**
2. **Remove all operational, tactical, and business-specific content**
3. **Preserve emotional intensity, clarity, and sharp positioning**
4. **Increase conviction and reduce ambiguity**

---

## 🧭 Definition: What a Manifesto Is (For This Project)

The manifesto should ONLY do the following:

### 1. Define the Worldview

* What is broken about current work / organizations / product building?

### 2. Define the Bet

* What do we believe instead?
* What are we building differently?

### 3. Define Identity

* Who this is for
* Who this is NOT for

### 4. Define Non-Negotiables

* Principles that cannot be compromised

### 5. Extend an Invitation

* A call to the right people

---

## 🚫 What a Manifesto Is NOT

The manifesto MUST NOT include:

* ❌ Business models (pricing, revenue streams)
* ❌ Service descriptions (consulting, build sprint, etc.)
* ❌ Operating structures (pods, stages, onboarding timelines)
* ❌ Compensation philosophy
* ❌ Tools / tech stack
* ❌ Internal experiments or frameworks
* ❌ Strategy notes or brainstorming content
* ❌ Detailed “how we work” execution

These must be extracted into separate documents.

## ✍️ Writing Guidelines

### Tone

* Bold, declarative, unapologetic
* Avoid corporate language
* Avoid “balanced” statements
* Prefer strong positions over safe phrasing

### Style

* Short paragraphs
* High signal, low explanation
* Minimal jargon
* No operational detail

### Content Rules

* If a sentence answers “HOW” → remove it
* If a sentence answers “WHY” → keep it
* If a sentence answers “WHAT WE OFFER” → remove it

---

## ⚠️ Critical Guardrails

* Do NOT dilute intensity in pursuit of clarity
* Do NOT turn this into a landing page
* Do NOT introduce new concepts not already implied
* Do NOT over-explain
